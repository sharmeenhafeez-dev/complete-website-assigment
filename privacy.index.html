Epic School: Our Privacy Promise

At Epic, we promise to uphold high standards of privacy for you and your students, so you can focus on what matters most: reading and learning!

No ads.

You won’t see third party advertising on Epic School.

We never sell your data.

Epic does not sell student data. We don’t use students' personal information for advertising or marketing.

We are COPPA and FERPA compliant.

Epic School is designed to comply with applicable federal and state children and student privacy laws, including COPPA and FERPA, and to help our schools meet their own compliance obligations. We partner with schools to help keep kids safe while they independently explore the Epic library and develop a love of reading and learning.

Only the data we need, nothing more.

Epic only collects the minimum personal information necessary to provide the best possible experience of the Epic School platform for both educators and students and we use this data only to provide our service to schools, students, and parents. For example, we may use data to allow teachers to monitor student progress, suggest content or assignments, or permit parents and guardians to review their children’s work.

Only teachers can create student accounts.

Students are not permitted to create an Epic School account. All students must be invited to an Epic classroom roster by a teacher or school administrator. Only teachers can enter student information, like first and last names and learning levels, but may choose to allow students to enter their own first name and last initial to their profile.

We use network security and encryption technology to protect student data.

Our dedicated team uses physical, technological and administrative security measures to help keep your information safe and protected.

We love transparency.

We’ll always be transparent about our practices and if anything changes, we’ll be sure to let you know.
Epic is the leading digital reading platform—built on a collection of 40,000+ popular, high-quality books from 250+ of the world’s best publishers—that safely fuels curiosity and reading confidence for kids 12 and under.
